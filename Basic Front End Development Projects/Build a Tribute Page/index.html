Batman: The Dark Knight

  • 1980 - "Born at Gotham City Hospital as Bruce Wayne.
  • 1989 - "Bruce Wayne witnesses his parents murder.
  • 1994 - "Bruce begins his global sojourn, dropping in and out of school. Picking up various martial arts skills on his travels."
  • 2005 - "Bruce dawns the mantle of Batman, fighting injustice that has run rampant in Gotham City."
  • 2008 - "Batman faces off with the Joker, an enemy that is born from the vacuum of crime lords. Batman blames himself for the new class of criminal that the joker represents. Thinking that the escalation of evil is the product of his vigilantism.
  • 2012 - "The ultimate challenge is faced by Batman, he had to come to terms with what he is and saves Gotham City from complete destruction. Facing off with the deceptive enemy Ra's al Ghuls daughter and defeating her at a great cost to the city.
  • Current - "It is unknown where Batman is, perhaps he will show up when he is most needed.
